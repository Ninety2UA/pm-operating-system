# Bid Strategy Decision Tree

## Context
Interactive decision tree that helps advertisers select the right Google Ads bid strategy based on their campaign objectives, maturity level, data volume, and constraints — with rationale at each node. Automates the strategic conversation had hundreds of times while consulting 25+ clients and scaling automation growth by 158%. Consulting gold — distills complex strategic decisions into a usable tool. Supports goal: build & ship products, become recognized expert, acquire clients.

Tech stack: React, simple state machine or decision tree logic, Tailwind, optional Mermaid diagram.

## Scope
- 6-8 decision nodes covering tROAS, tCPA, Max Conversions, Max Conv Value, and manual CPC
- 3-4 qualifying questions at each branch
