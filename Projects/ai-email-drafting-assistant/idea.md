# AI Email Drafting Assistant with Strategy Options

## Context
Describe the email situation (context, recipient, goal) and the tool generates 2-3 strategically different drafts — each optimized for a different outcome (e.g., "push for alignment" vs. "disagree and commit"). Supports goal: build & ship products, develop AI product skills.

Tech stack: React, Claude API, variant rendering with labeled strategies.

## Scope
- Form with context, recipient role, and goal
- Claude generates 3 labeled drafts
- Each draft shows strategy name, tradeoff note, and full email text
