# Marketing Experiment Tracker

## Context
Dashboard for tracking A/B tests and marketing experiments — with statistical significance calculator, timeline view, and outcome logging. A/B testing and experiment-driven optimization were core to performance marketing work across all roles. Shows structured thinking about experimentation. Supports goal: build & ship products, build custom analytical solutions.

Tech stack: React, statistical significance calculator (Z-test in JS), Recharts, persistent storage API.

## Scope
- Experiment card (name, hypothesis, metrics, status)
- Built-in significance calculator (input control/variant numbers)
- Timeline view of running experiments
- Win/loss tagging
